Why do I want to take part?

I want to participate in the summer school because I like to learn new things that interest me. I have heard of most of the things mentioned on the landing page but never actually used them in a real project - it's time to change that and to become more fluent in these techniques. And it's always easier to learn in a group and with the help of experts - who can gently push you in the right direction - than to learn all alone
Another point is that I like to become more confident in my own abilities. I know I am not totally stupid and I have some abilities but when it comes to 'selling' them or showing them I suddenly feel like I'm not good enough for this job because there might occur a problem I haven't thought of yet and therefore can't solve.
I also like to meet people who have the same passion as me to build helpful relationships for the future.
The last reason is my bachelor thesis (which includdes coding). I am writing about AWS and headless CMS and I feel like I could learn a lot about these things during summer school.
